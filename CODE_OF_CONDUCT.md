# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want a harassment-free, respectful experience for everyone who interacts with this
project — contributors, users, and maintainers alike.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and considerate. Assume good intent; disagree on ideas, not people.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Respect the privacy and financial data of users — never share or misuse it.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sharing real tenant data, secrets, or credentials in issues, PRs, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behaviour to the maintainer at https://hisabkitab.pro. Reports are
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove content, or block contributors, that
violates this code.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), v2.1.
