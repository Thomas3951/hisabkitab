# HisabKitab — Terms of Service

_Last updated: 2026-06-16. Plain-language summary; not legal advice. For a Nepal-registered
business using HisabKitab over WhatsApp._

## 1. What HisabKitab is
HisabKitab is a bookkeeping and VAT/TDS **preparation assistant** for small VAT-registered
businesses in Nepal, delivered over WhatsApp. It helps you record sales and expenses, read
bills, compute VAT/TDS, track receivables/payables, and prepare figures and reports.

## 2. What HisabKitab is NOT — the important part
- **It is not a licensed auditor and does not provide statutory sign-off.** Figures it
  prepares are assistance, not certified accounts.
- **It never files anything with the government.** It prepares numbers; **you review and file**
  with the IRD yourself. HisabKitab never logs into any government portal.
- **It never claims to be error-free.** It is built so that nothing is saved or stated as a
  financial figure without your confirmation, it shows its work, and it asks when unsure — but
  you remain responsible for the accuracy of what you confirm and file.

## 3. Your responsibilities
- Provide accurate information and review every draft before confirming it.
- Keep your own copies of source documents (bills, invoices).
- File returns and pay taxes on time. Deadlines shown are computed/estimated; **verify them on
  the IRD calendar.**
- Keep your WhatsApp number secure. Never send passwords, OTPs, or portal logins in chat —
  HisabKitab refuses these by design.

## 4. Confirmation & money actions
Every entry is a **draft** until you explicitly confirm it. No payment or refund happens
without your explicit approval for that specific action.

## 5. Subscription & billing
HisabKitab is a prepaid monthly subscription (Starter / Pro / Business). Prices are shown
before you pay. A lapsed subscription keeps read access during a short grace period, then
suspends; your data is retained, not deleted, and paying reactivates. Cancel anytime; access
continues until the end of the paid period. See the Billing section in-app.

## 6. Fair use & limits
Each plan has monthly usage limits to protect service quality. Heavy or abusive use may be
throttled (you are told; nothing you sent is lost). HisabKitab answers questions about **your
business's accounts** and politely declines unrelated requests.

request deletion of your business's data at any time.
